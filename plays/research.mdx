## Default Research
By default, Playmaker will research your prospect's website, social media, and news mentions. It will also search the web for information about the company. All of this reseach is filtered to the most relevant and passed to the writer.

## Custom Research Tasks
Your AI rep can use the internet to find specific information for outreach. 

Navigate to the **Research** tab, and click `Add research focus`.

Select between company-level or contact-level research.

You can test the research task using the `Test it` button to preview results.
