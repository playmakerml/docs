## Basic Research

Playmaker will research your prospect's website, social media, and news mentions by default. It will also search the web for information about the company.&#x20;

## Custom Research

Your AI rep can use the internet to find specific information for outreach.

Navigate to the **Research** tab, and click `Add research focus`.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://cdn.playmakerdev.com/images/docs/research.jpg"
/>

Select between company-level or contact-level research.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://cdn.playmakerdev.com/images/docs/research-type.jpg"
/>





You can test the research task using the `Test it` button to preview results.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://cdn.playmakerdev.com/images/docs/research-test.jpg"
/>
